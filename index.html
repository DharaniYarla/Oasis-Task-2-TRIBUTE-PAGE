SudhaMurty

Sudha Murthy is an Indian author, educator, and philanthropist who is the chairperson of the Infosys Foundation. She has won many awards for her work in education, social welfare, and literature, including the Padma Shri in 2006 and the R.K. Narayan Award for Literature in 2006. She has written over 30 books and has made a significant impact through her philanthropic work. Sudha Murthy is an inspiration to many and her contributions to Indian society will continue to inspire future generations.
